Health Mate

The Problem

Many people experience symptoms and need quick answers about their health. It's challenging to differentiate between diseases when symptoms overlap.

The Solution

We developed a web app that allows users to input their symptoms, and based on a pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier model, the app predicts the most likely disease. Here's how it works:

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter their symptoms via a user-friendly interface, supporting a wide range of symptoms.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Decision Tree Classifier trained on symptom-disease data predicts possible health conditions.
  3. Prediction: The app provides instant disease predictions based on input symptoms.
  4. Additional Information: It also offers disease descriptions, medications, dietary advice, and workout tips.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy navigation for symptom input.
  • Accurate Predictions: The machine learning model provides reliable predictions.
  • Comprehensive Information: The app includes disease descriptions, medications, diets, and workout tips.
  • Educational Content: Informative blog posts about various health topics empower users.

Conclusion

Our app brings the power of machine learning and healthcare information to users, offering a valuable resource for quick health insights.
